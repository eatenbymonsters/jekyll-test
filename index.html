I'm a web designer and developer based in Cornwall, UK.

I believe in building websites that are accessible. All devices, platforms, and users should get the same content and the same functionality.

I also believe in aesthetics; simplicity of form and function is valuable and important, but simple doesn't have to mean ugly. Careful use of typography, photography, and illustration (ography?) can produce beautiful designs that are as easy and intuitive to use as they are visually pleasing. User Experience and graphic style go hand-in-hand.

Browse my portfolio to see some recent examples of my work, and read more of my thoughts on designing for the web on my blog. To kickstart your project email me at me@thomashazledine.com; I'm always happy to answer any questions.
